Layered coffee flavored biscuits with marscapone cream

Ingredients

  • 1 pack ladyfinger biscuits
  • 1 cup strong coffee
  • 1 cup marscapone
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder to dust on top

Directions

  1. Whip the heavycream until stiff peaks in a bowl
  2. fold in the marscapone, and vanilla extract to the whipped cream
  3. dip the ladyfinger biscuits in the coffee for a couple seconds each side
  4. get a large tray and start with a layer of ladyfingers, then around 1/3 of the cream mixutre
  5. repeat for 3 more layers
  6. dust top with cocoa powder, and set in the fridge overnight
